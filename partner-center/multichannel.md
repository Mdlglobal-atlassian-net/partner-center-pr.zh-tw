# <a name="multi-channel-support"></a>多通路支援

**適用於**

-  合作夥伴中心
-  Microsoft Cloud for US Government 適用的合作夥伴中心


The Partner Center's multi-channel feature supports scenarios when a customer wants to hire a partner to manage and support a subscription they purchased elsewhere. 多通路支援適用於當客戶：

-   已經直接從 Microsoft，或透過顧問、Open 或 EA 方案購買訂閱。

-   從第三方購買訂閱，第三方不屬於雲端解決方案提供者計畫或非 Microsoft 同步發佈合作夥伴。 Customers can't switch existing license-based subscriptions to a different partner in the Cloud Solution Provider program-they must wait until the end of their subscription period or cancel before moving.
