## <a href="" id="licensebased"></a><span data-ttu-id="4f118-124">授權型帳單</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-124">License-based billing</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4f118-126">**週年日：**採用每月計費的訂閱會與合作夥伴帳單日期校準，而採用年度計費的訂閱則與購買日期校準。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-126">**Anniversary Day:** Subscriptions with monthly billing are aligned to the partner billing date and subscriptions with annual billing are aligned to the purchase date.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4f118-127">**免費期間：**採用每月計費的訂閱擁有免費期間，從購買日期到下一個合作夥伴帳單日期之間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-127">**Free Period:** Subscriptions with monthly billing are given a free period from the purchase date to the next partner billing date.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4f118-128">**期間：**所有授權型訂閱都擁有 12 個月付費期間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-128">**Term:** All license-based subscriptions have a 12-month paid term.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4f118-129">年度計費訂閱的付費期間從購買日期開始計算，每月計費訂閱的付費期間從購買日之後的帳單日期開始計算。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-129">The paid term begins on the purchase date for annual billing subscriptions and on the billing date following the purchase date for monthly billing subscriptions.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4f118-130">**取消：**若在付費期間的頭 30 天內取消訂閱，將可收到 100% 的退款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-130">**Cancellation:** Subscriptions cancelled in the first 30 days of the paid term will be credited 100%.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4f118-131">30 天後取消的訂閱則會收到依比例計算的退款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-131">Subscriptions cancelled after 30 days will be credited on a prorated basis.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4f118-132">**續約：**所有授權型訂閱都在付費期間開始的 12 個月後自動續約。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f118-132">**Renewal:** All license-based subscriptions auto-renew 12-months after the paid term begins.</span></span>
