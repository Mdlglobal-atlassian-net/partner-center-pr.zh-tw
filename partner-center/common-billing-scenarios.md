## <a href="" id="licensebased"></a><span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-123">授權型帳單</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-123">License-based billing</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-124">**帳單：**授權型訂閱會在訂閱週年日，事先收取整個期間的費用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-124">**Billing:** License-based subscriptions are billed in advance on the subscription anniversary day.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-125">**週年日：**採用每月計費的訂閱會與合作夥伴帳單日期校準，而採用年度計費的訂閱則與購買日期校準。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-125">**Anniversary Day:** Subscriptions with monthly billing are aligned to the partner billing date and subscriptions with annual billing are aligned to the purchase date.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-126">**免費期間：**採用每月計費的訂閱擁有免費期間，從購買日期到下一個合作夥伴帳單日期之間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-126">**Free Period:** Subscriptions with monthly billing are given a free period from the purchase date to the next partner billing date.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-129">**取消：**若在付費期間的頭 30 天內取消訂閱，將可收到 100% 的退款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-129">**Cancellation:** Subscriptions cancelled in the first 30 days of the paid term will be credited 100%.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-130">30 天後取消的訂閱則會收到依比例計算的退款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-130">Subscriptions cancelled after 30 days will be credited on a prorated basis.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="0f2c2-131">**續約：**所有授權型訂閱都在付費期間開始的 12 個月後自動續約。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0f2c2-131">**Renewal:** All license-based subscriptions auto-renew 12-months after the paid term begins.</span></span>
