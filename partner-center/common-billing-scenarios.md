<span data-ttu-id="eb6cc-126">**免費期間：**採用每月計費的訂閱擁有免費期間，從購買日期到下一個合作夥伴帳單日期之間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb6cc-126">**Free Period:** Subscriptions with monthly billing are given a free period from the purchase date to the next partner billing date.</span></span>
